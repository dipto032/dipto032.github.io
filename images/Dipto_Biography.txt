Biography - Dipto

Dipobrato Sarbapalli finished his B.Tech degree from the Department of Civil Engineering at National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirappalli (India) in 2015, and his M.S. from the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in 2018. His Masters thesis under Professor Paramita Mondal was focused on understanding nucleation kinetics and reaction mechanisms of sustainable, alternative, aluminosilicate binder materials termed as geopolymers. He is a doctoral student under Prof. Joaquín Rodríguez-López and is currently working on the characterization of alkali-ion intercalation within graphene.
